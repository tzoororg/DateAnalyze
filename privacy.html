Privacy Policy

Us — a private date-tracking app. Effective 2026-07-20.

Summary: your date entries stay on your device by default. Optional partner sync is end-to-end encrypted; we cannot read your content.

Local data

By default, everything you log — entries, ratings, notes, photos — is stored only in your browser's local database (IndexedDB) on your device. No account is required. Nothing leaves your device unless you turn on partner sync.

Optional sync (Firebase)

If you turn on sync, you sign in with Google and we store your email/user ID to identify your account. Your date and photo data is stored in Firestore, but it's encrypted on your device first (AES-256-GCM) before it's ever sent — the encryption key never leaves your and your partner's devices. As the app operator, we can only see entry counts, timestamps, and which devices belong to your shared space — never your titles, notes, ratings, or photos.

Photos

Photos are stored on your device. If sync is on, they're also stored as encrypted documents in Firestore so your partner can see them too.

Crash reports

If the app crashes, we may receive the error message, stack trace, and filename — never your entry content. You can opt out by setting crashReports to "off" in your browser's local storage.

Feedback

The in-app "Send feedback" form sends your message and any attached photo to a Cloudflare Worker, which files a GitHub issue so it can be reviewed.

Push notifications

If enabled, notifications are delivered via Firebase Cloud Messaging with fixed, generic text (e.g. "your partner logged a date") — no entry content is included.

No analytics, no ads, no trackers

We don't run analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts of any kind.

Data deletion

You can erase everything from the ⋯ menu in the app at any time. To permanently delete your account, use ⋯ menu → "Delete account": this removes your Google sign-in and — if your partner isn't in the shared space — all shared dates and photos, plus all data stored on the device. This action cannot be undone.
